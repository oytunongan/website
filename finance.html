Financial Analysis Application

Fetches raw company data from SEC EDGAR APIs, processes fiscal-year–based financial statements (non-TTM), and computes financial ratios. Machine learning models are then trained and tested on historical data to predict a stock’s intrinsic and future value. However, due to noise in the raw data, the predictions may be unreliable and potentially outdated (based on fiscal-year data). Therefore, these results should not be considered investment advice or used for investment decisions. Type the company’s ticker symbol below to try it out.
